  TermFrequency
  =============
    TermFrequency is the number of times a term appears in a document.
    It is used in information retrieval and text mining to determine the relevance of a document to a search query.
    The higher the term frequency, the more relevant the document is likely to be to the search query.
    But!!, term frequency alone is not sufficient to determine the relevance of a document.
    If the term appears frequently in a field in a lengthy text, it may not be as relevant as a document where the term appears less frequently but in a more concise text.

        1 occurrence → big score boost
        2 occurrences → smaller boost
        3 occurrences → tiny boost
        10 occurrences → almost no extra benefit
